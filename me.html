About Me

I am a 3rd year Computer Engineering undergraduate student at The University of Hong Kong.

I have worked as an Engineer in many tech-based Oceanographic and Environmental projects such as Cesar Harada's Protei and WWF's Marine Litter Detective. Currently, I am co-organizing MindoRobots, a research project which aims to map Coral Reefs around the island of Mindoro, Philippines using a Swarm of Oceanographic Robots.

I love teaching myself new technologies and code from the diverse fields of IoT, Signal Processing, Mobile Application & Web Development, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

At HKU, I'm a Teaching Assistant for the course COMP2123: Programming Technologies and Tools. I'm also a Guitarist, currently a part of The Zero Point fusion ensemble.
